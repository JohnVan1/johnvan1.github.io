DeeBoT (Berkeley DBT)

An innovative AI chatbot built on top of OpenAI's GPT-4 architecture that incorporates the fundamental skills of dialectical behavior therapy (DBT). Designed to serve as your personal DBT therapist, DeeBoT delivers customized guidance tailored by your own individual story and emotions, fostering a more personalized and deep therapy experience.

This project aims to demonstrate how new developments in NLP can be utilized in mental health education, particularly in making DBT more accessible. We hope to contribute substantially to the field of mental health services, offering practical solutions that enhance user experience and accessibility.

Fraudulent Job Postings (NLP)

Built a fake job posting detector using a suite of natural language processing techniques to identify fraudulent listings and protect job seekers.

The project compares three approaches—TF-IDF with classical machine learning models, GloVe embeddings, and BERT—to understand trade-offs in interpretability, performance, and compute cost for real-world deployment.
